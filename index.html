About Us

Parkside Baptist Church is a family of born-again believers that meets regularly for worship and prayer, missions and evangelism, discipleship and teaching, and fellowship on one of the busiest corners in the growing Maritime city of Moncton, New Brunswick, Canada.

Check out the website for more information about times, location, staff, ministries and other events going on throughout the year.

Our Ministries

Here at Parkside Baptist Church, we offer a wide range of ministries. We have a program for every age.

Children's Ministry

Special programs for children with age-appropriate Bible teaching.

Youth Ministry

Activities and Bible studies aimed at teenagers and young adults.

Adult Ministry

Bible studies, prayer groups, and activities for adults of all ages.

Music Ministry

Church choir and musical groups that lead worship in services.

Missions Ministry

Support for missionaries and local and international evangelistic projects.

Visitation Ministry

Pastoral care and visitation to members and the local community.
